EHRI-UK

Creating a UK National Holocaust Research Infrastructure

The UK holds a unique collection of records relating to the Holocaust and refugees who fled Nazi oppression. EHRI-UK is working towards finding and linking this source material in the UK through a national digital Holocaust Research Infrastructure. EHRI-UK is the UK national node of the European Holocaust Research Infrastructure (EHRI), a European Union funded project which in the process of becoming a permanent pan-European organisation.
